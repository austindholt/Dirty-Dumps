Quick FAQ

We can usually give a good estimate from photos. Final price is confirmed on-site before loading anything.

What areas do you serve?

Boise, Meridian, Garden City, Eagle, Kuna, Nampa, Caldwell, and nearby Treasure Valley areas depending on the job.

Not without the proper disposal route and approval. We do not knowingly haul prohibited or hazardous materials.

Can you remove appliances?

Yes, in many cases. Specialty disposal fees may apply depending on the item and location.
